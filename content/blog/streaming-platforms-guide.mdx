# The Ultimate Streaming Platform Guide for 2025

With so many streaming services available, choosing the right combination can be overwhelming. Here's our comprehensive guide to help you decide.

## Platform Comparison

### Netflix
- **Price**: $6.99-$22.99/month
- **Content**: Original series, movies, documentaries
- **Best For**: Binge-watching, international content

### Prime Video
- **Price**: $8.99/month (or included with Prime)
- **Content**: Movies, TV shows, Amazon Originals
- **Best For**: Movie library, value with Prime benefits

### Disney+
- **Price**: $7.99/month
- **Content**: Disney, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic
- **Best For**: Family content, franchises
